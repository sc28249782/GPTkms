`pages/` holds refined knowledge for future reuse.

`raw/` stores source captures and should remain immutable.

`inbox/` is a staging zone before cleanup and organization.

`snapshots/` is useful for periodic exports and summaries.

See also [Missing page example](../concepts/does-not-exist.md).
